## Note template

```markdown
---
title: ...
tags: [codex, skill, openspec]
status: draft
verified: false
---

# ...

## 适用场景

## 已验证事实

## 当前假设

## 待验证事项

## 可复用模板

## 后续行动

## 不要误用
```

## Rules

- Do not present assumptions as verified facts.
- Preserve commands, validation results, risks, and rollback notes.
- Do not over-compress long-term knowledge notes.
